About Me
Hi, my names Jose. I’m 25 years, born and raised in Arizona. I’ve always had an interest in technology and design which led me to web development. While I’ve never considered myself a “tech guy” and an “artist”, growing up I always enjoyed art and computers. Slowly as the internet has evolved, I’ve seen website designs change as well. I am a full stack developer specializing and working on HTML 5, CSS, JavaScript and NodeJS. I’m currently working on my craft to make a career change into a growing industry.
